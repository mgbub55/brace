Remote Deposit

Deposit Checks Remotely, without Leaving the Office

Remote Deposit is a safe, secure and efficient way to process checks received at your place of business, without running to the bank.

Contact Us

Step 1: Scan

Using the desktop check scanner we provide, you can quickly scan both sides of checks in a single pass. We accommodate highly accurate batch scans and allow you to view images of scanned checks right from your computer.

Step 2: Transmit

Your scanned check amounts are automatically read and deposits are totaled for you on the spot. When you're ready to make a deposit, follow our simple instructions to transmit from your computer! You can make your deposit any time, day or night, through a secure Internet connection.

Step 3: Deposit

First PREMIER Bank receives and processes your check images quickly and efficiently, applying later deposit deadlines so your funds become available faster. Payments received by 6pm Central Time are deposited same-day. Payments received after 6pm will be deposited the next business day.

﻿

The Benefits of Remote Deposit

  • Perfect for high volume / high dollar deposits
  • Easy-to-use scanner and software
  • Secure and automated system
  • Time-saving, accurate and efficient way to deposit funds
  • Process large volumes of checks easily
  • No manual preparation of deposits
  • No need for trips to the bank
  • Fast funds deposit improves cashflow
